About Me

At early age I grew interest in computers. They were and still fascinate me in how they work. The events that lead to my decision of what specific aspect of computers I was interested in was during junior and high school years. In 8th grade I job shadowed my cousin that at the time was the editor of Lewis-Clark State College website. I enjoyed my experience so when I was offered a web development course in high school, I jumped on it.

In high school besides the web development class I also enrolled in two business application courses where I learned advanced features of Microsoft office, specifically Word, PowerPoint, Excel, and Access.

At Lewis-Clark State College some highlight courses are Intro/Computer Program. In this course I learned JavaScript and my favorite unit was building a subtraction math game. The game was my midterm and it really let me play around and I had a few aha moments making it. Another highlight was Math for Technology which I learned about………. The reason this sticks out is because it really got my brain thinking and helped me improve my thought process.
